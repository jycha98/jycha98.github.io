Jungyoub Cha

M.S. Student · Seoul National University

Hello, my name is Jungyoub Cha, and I am currently pursuing an M.S. at Big Data AI Center, Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence, Seoul National University. I earned my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from KAIST, with a minor in Business and Technology Management. I have also previously interned at NAVER Corp. and KAIST AI.

My research focuses on efficient inference for large language models (LLMs), with an emphasis on system-level and algorithmic optimization. I work on decoding algorithms, KV cache management, and LLM architectures to make LLMs faster and more cost-efficient.

More recently, I have been exploring improving reasoning efficiency via latent reasoning and developing acceleration frameworks for diffusion-based language models, with the goal of making emerging generative paradigms more practical under real-world computational constraints.

My long-term goal is to make powerful language models more broadly accessible, not only to large corporations but also to individual researchers, educators, and resource-constrained labs.
