Hello, my name is Jungyoub Cha, and I am currently pursuing an M.S. in the Big Data AI Center at Seoul National University. I earned my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from KAIST, with a minor in Business and Technology Management. I have also interned at NAVER Media Intelligence and KAIST AI.

My research interests center on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Natural Language Processing. I am particularly interested in Large Language Model (LLM) efficiency and inference optimization, aiming to make LLMs faster, more resource‑efficient, and widely accessible for real‑world applications.
