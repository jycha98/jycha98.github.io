Hello, my name is Jungyoub Cha, and I am currently pursuing an M.S. at Big Data AI Center, Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence, Seoul National University. I earned my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from KAIST, with a minor in Business and Technology Management. I have also interned at NAVER Corp. and KAIST AI.

My research interests span Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, with a particular focus on Large Language Model (LLM) efficiency and inference optimization. I am especially interested in developing methods to make LLMs faster, more resource-efficient, and broadly accessible for real-world applications.
